Features

  • Test Android APKs directly in your web browser.
  • Secure and sandboxed environment for safe testing.
  • Explore app functionality without installing on your device.
  • Easy-to-use drag-and-drop APK upload.
  • Switch between light and dark themes for comfortable testing.

Get Started

  1. Drag your APK file and drop it onto the screen.
  2. Click the "Get Started" button to begin testing.
  3. Interact with the app as if it were installed on your device.
  4. Explore features and functionality with ease.
  5. Switch between themes for the best testing experience.
